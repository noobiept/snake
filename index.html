Help

Food : adds a tail to the snake.
Double Food : adds two tails to the snake.
Wall : You can't go through it.

In two player mode, wins the player
which has more tails, or if the other
player collides against your snake.

Player 1 keyboard (green)

  • up : w
  • down : s
  • left : a
  • right : d

Player 2 keyboard (blue)

  • up : up arrow
  • down : down arrow
  • left : left arrow
  • right : right arrow
In single player mode, you can play with either the player 1 or player 2 keys.
